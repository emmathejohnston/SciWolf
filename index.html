Sci Wolf

Interactive science simulations for curious minds. Pick a topic and start exploring.

⚗️
Chemical Equation Balancer
Practice balancing chemical equations by adjusting coefficients. A live atom-count table shows whether reactants and products are in balance.

⚛️
Bohr-Rutherford Diagram
Build atoms by adding protons, neutrons, and electrons. The nucleus and electron shells update live, and the element name, mass, and charge are shown automatically.
Open simulation ›
⚡
Static Electricity
Rub balloons, fur, glass rods, and more against each other to transfer electrons. Watch charged objects attract and repel, ground charges, and deflect an electroscope.
Open simulation ›
🔭
Ray Diagram Simulator
Explore how lenses and mirrors form images by tracing light rays. Drag the object and focal points to see real and virtual images appear in real time.
Open simulation ›
🧪
pH Indicator Lab
Drag household substances into a 12-well spot plate, then apply pH indicators to observe colour changes. Use the digital pH meter to measure any well directly.
Open simulation ›
🔬
Inner Life of a Cell
You are a mitochondrion. Move through a living cell with WASD and donate ATP to organelles to power their functions. Explore the nucleus, Golgi, ER, lysosomes, and more.
Open simulation ›
🌳
Forest Succession
Walk through a living landscape as ecological succession unfolds over centuries. Pioneer weeds give way to scrub, then woodland, then ancient forest. Click to learn, eat to survive — and when you die, you become part of the forest.
